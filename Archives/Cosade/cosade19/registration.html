Registration

Registration Details

The registration fee is 370 € (270 € for students). This fee includes welcome reception, lunches, coffee breaks and the conference dinner. Hotel rooms need to be booked separately.

If you register until 3rd March 2019 you benefit from an early-bird registration discount! The early-bird registration fee is 320 € (220 € for students).

Registration Fees

Students

  • Early bird (until 3rd March 2019): 220 €
  • After 3rd March 2019: 270 €

Non Students

  • Early bird (until 3rd March 2019 ): 320 €
  • After 3rd March 2019: 370 €

Registration Rules

  • Registration is possible until March 29th, 2019.
  • No on-site registration is available.
  • Only the payment by credit card.
  • An invoice will be available after the registration procedure.
  • Please note: No reimbursement will be possible.

Registration Site

Register online via the registration form.

You have to create an account and complete a form before going to the payment site. It is recommended to complete all the fields of the form, even if not mandatory.
