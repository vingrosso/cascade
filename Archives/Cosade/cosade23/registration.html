Registration Details

Two types of registrations are available for COSADE 2023:

  • On-site: the registration fee includes reception, lunches, coffee breaks and the conference dinner. Hotel rooms need to be booked separately.

  • Virtual: the registration fee includes online participation via Microsoft Teams or similar.

If you register before February 28, 2023, you benefit from an early-bird registration discount.

All presentations have to be given on-site. Thus, one author of each accepted paper is required to register for on-site participation.

Registration is possible until March 17, 2023.

On-site registration with cash payment is not available.

Registration fees can be paid by credit card or wire transfer.
