Registration

Registration Details

Two types of registrations are available for COSADE 2024:

  1. On-site: the registration fee covers on-site conference participation, reception, lunches, coffee breaks, and the conference dinner. Please note that hotel rooms must be booked separately.
  2. Virtual: the registration fee includes online participation via Microsoft Teams or similar.

If you register before March 12, 2024, you benefit from an early-bird registration discount.

All presentations have to be given on-site. Thus, one author of each accepted paper is required to register for on-site participation.

Registration is possible until April 02, 2024.

On-site registration with cash payment is not available.

Registration fees can be paid by credit card or wire transfer.


Registration Site

Registering is done using Easychair, please click on the following link to continue: https://easychair.org/my/conference?conf=cosade2024
