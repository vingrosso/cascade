Registration

Registration Details

The registration fee is 330,00 € (192,50  € for students). This fee includes lunches, coffee breaks and the conference dinner. Hotel rooms need to be booked separately.

If you register until 28th February 2025 you benefit from an early-bird registration discount! The early-bird registration fee is 275 € (137,50 € for students).

Registration Fees

Students

  • Early bird (until 28th February 2025): 137,50 €
  • After 28th February 2025: 192,50 €

Non Students

  • Early bird (until 28th Feruary 2025 ): 275 €
  • After 28th February 2025: 330 €

Registration Rules

  • Registration is possible until March 31st, 2025.
  • No on-site registration is available.
  • Registration fees can be paid by credit card or wire transfer.
  • Please note: No reimbursement will be possible.

Registration Site
