Call for sponsors

From March 30 - April 1, 2026, CASCADE will be organized by the Chair of Data Security and Cryptography at the Faculty of Informatics and Data Science at the University of Regensburg, Germany. If you are interested in a sponsorship, please contact the CASCADE local organizers juliane.kraemer@ur.de .

Sponsoring CASCADE 2026

CASCADE welcomes sponsors to support this event. We offer three different sponsorship packages

Silver Sponsor (1000 € + VAT)

  • your company logo will be featured as a sponsor on the CASCADE 2026 website
  • your company will be acknowledged in the conference proceedings
  • you will be offered an opportunity to include materials or gifts in registration packages

Golden Sponsor (2500 € + VAT)

  • you will be acknowledged in the General Chair’s opening remarks
  • you will be offered space for an exhibit booth
  • you will be offered two free admissions to the conference
  • your company logo will be featured as a sponsor on the CASCADE 2026 website
  • your company will be acknowledged in the conference proceedings
  • you will be offered an opportunity to include materials or gifts in registration packages

Platinum Sponsorship Package (5000 € + VAT)

  • you will have the opportunity for additional exclusive promotion opportunities (fixed individually with each platinum sponsor, examples include the placement of banners in the conference hall, on menu cards, on the staff T-shirts)
  • you will be acknowledged in the General Chair’s opening remarks
  • you will be offered space for an exhibit booth
  • you will be offered two free admissions to the conference
  • your company logo will be featured as a sponsor on the CASCADE 2026 website
  • your company will be acknowledged in the conference proceedings
  • you will be offered an opportunity to include materials or gifts in registration packages

If you are interested in a sponsorship, please contact the CASCADE local organizers:
