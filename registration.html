Registration

Registration Details

The registration fee is 330 € (190 € for students). This fee includes the welcome reception, lunches, coffee breaks, a guided tour, and the conference dinner. Hotel rooms need to be booked separately.

If you register until 28th February 2026 you benefit from an early-bird registration discount! The early-bird registration fee is 270 € (135 € for students).

Registration Fees

Students

  • Early bird (until 28th February 2026): 135 €
  • After 28th February 2026: 190 €

Non Students

  • Early bird (until 28th February 2026): 270 €
  • After 28th February 2026: 330 €

Registration Rules

  • Registration is possible until March 25st, 2026.
  • No on-site registration is available.
  • Registration fees can be paid by credit card or wire transfer.
  • Please note: No reimbursement will be possible.

Registration Site

Register online via the widget below or using the registration site.
